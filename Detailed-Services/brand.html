About Us

Leads the investigation, analysis and development of business, communication and brand strategies. By combining his creative credentials with the analytical thought processes of strategic brand positioning, he has successfully developed a reputation for driving innovative brand communications in both the B2B and B2C arenas. Our Brand Director has built a livelihood deep-rooted in communications and has given rise to many stellar communication projects. He draws on fifteen years of experience working for revered brand consultancies in the United Arab Emirates and in fraught publicity environments in the United Kingdom public and private sectors; most notably the BBC (British Broadcasting Corporation). Over the years, in his capacity as a brand consultant, he has inspired and challenged a diverse range of clients. Today he continues his climb in the communications world by acting as an advisor for globally renowned brands, such as Sharjah Government; Sharjah Commerce and Tourism Development Authority; Dubai Municipality Staff Biographies and Sharjah Media Centre.
